Hamburger Recipe

Hamburger Ready to Eat

Here is a hamburger after being grilled and is ready to eat

Ingredients

  • Hamburger Buns
  • Ground Beef patties
  • Sliced Cheese
  • Toppings (Ketchup, Lettuce, Tomato, Pickles, Onions, Anything you want! )

Steps

  1. Step 1: Fire up the grill
  2. Step 2: Salt the patties
  3. Step 3: Grill patties until inside is slightly pink, you can make them as rare or well done as you please
  4. Step 4: Add cheese to top of patty on grill if desired
  5. Step 5: Toast buns on grill for approximately 20-30 seconds
  6. Step 6: Put patty on buns and add toppings of your choice
