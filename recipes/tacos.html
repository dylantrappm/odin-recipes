Tacos Recipe

Tacos are served!

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Carne Asada
  • Cheese
  • Toppings (Salsa, lettuce, onion, tomato, sour cream)

Steps

  1. Step 1: Cook the carne asada on the grill and add salt and seasonings of your choice
  2. Step 2: Cut up the carne asada on a cutting board into small pieces
  3. Step 3: Heat up tortillas on the grill until hot
  4. Step 4: Add carne asada to torilla and add desired toppings
