Lasagna Recipe


Lasagna Dish


Here is an example of a finished lasagna dish served on a plate ready to eat

Ingredients:

  • Pasta Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Ground Beef

Steps

  1. Step 1: Mix the diffent cheeses together in large mixing bowl
  2. Step 2: In 13x9 baking dish, layer first layer of pasta
  3. Step 3: Layer cooked ground beef on top of first layer of pasta
  4. Step 4: Layer of ground beef on top of cheese
  5. Step 5: Repeat steps 3 and 4 2 more times
  6. Step 6: Cover last layer of pasta with cheese
  7. Step 7: Cook at 350 for 75 minutes
  8. Step 8: Enjoy!
